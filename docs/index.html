Privacy Policy ChessLan: Offline Multiplayer Effective date: July 15, 2026 ChessLan: Offline Multiplayer is developed by Himesh Pant. This app is designed for offline/local multiplayer chess between nearby players. Developer Contact For privacy questions, contact: hpchess23@gmail.com Information We Collect ChessLan: Offline Multiplayer does not collect personal information from users. The app does not require users to create an account, sign in, or provide personal details such as name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location. Information Stored on Your Device The app may store basic app data locally on your device, such as settings, preferences, saved game state, or match history. This information stays on your device and is not uploaded to a developer-operated server. Local Multiplayer If you use local multiplayer features, the app may exchange game connection information and chess move data between nearby devices over local Wi-Fi, hotspot, Bluetooth, or another local connection method supported by the app. This information is used only to make the local chess match work. It is not sent to a developer-operated server. Ads and Analytics ChessLan: Offline Multiplayer does not show ads. ChessLan: Offline Multiplayer does not use analytics SDKs to track users. Data Sharing The developer does not sell, rent, or share personal data with advertisers, analytics providers, or other third parties. Permissions The app may use network, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or nearby device permissions only for local multiplayer connection features. The app does not request contacts, camera, microphone, SMS, call logs, photos, precise location, or advertising ID. Security Local app data is stored on your device. Local multiplayer data is exchanged only with devices you choose to connect with. You should play only with people you trust and on trusted local connections. Data Retention and Deletion Local app data remains on your device until you clear the app’s storage, delete saved games, or uninstall the app. Because the app does not create online accounts or store personal data on a developer-operated server, there is no server-side account or server-side personal data to delete. Children’s Privacy ChessLan: Offline Multiplayer does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not require accounts or personal details, no personal data is intentionally collected from children or adults. Your Choices You can stop using the app at any time. You can clear local app data from Android settings or uninstall the app. Changes to This Policy This privacy policy may be updated if the app changes. Updates will be posted with a new effective date.
